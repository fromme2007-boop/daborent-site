Pricing

DaboRent is free software for the property owner who uses it to manage their property.

What tenants pay

Tenants do not pay any fee to use DaboRent. The only money a tenant pays through DaboRent is their own monthly rent and their share of electricity & water, owed to their property owner.

ItemAmount
App usage fee for tenantsFree
App usage fee for the property ownerFree
Monthly rentAs agreed in the tenant's rental agreement (typically ₹3,000–₹15,000)
Electricity / water shareActual usage, split per room from sub-meter readings

The exact rent and utility amounts are set by the property owner and shown to each tenant in their monthly statement before any payment is made.
