DaboRent

Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated 25 May 2026

DaboRent (the “app”) is a private tenant–landlord management tool used by a small property owner (“the landlord”) to track rent, utility bills, and payments for their tenants (“you”, “the tenant”). This privacy policy explains what information the app collects, how it’s used, and your rights.

1. Who runs the app

The app is operated by the landlord who invited you to it. Tenants are added by the landlord — there is no public sign-up. For privacy questions, contact your landlord directly.

3. How we use the information

  • Show you what you owe and your payment history in the app
  • Send you WhatsApp reminders when a payment is due or overdue
  • Generate Razorpay payment links when you choose to pay online
  • Help your landlord manage the property (rooms, occupancy, utility bills, settlement)
  • Diagnose technical problems if the app crashes (via Sentry, if enabled)

We do not:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Use it for advertising
  • Share it with anyone other than your landlord, the service providers listed below, or as required by law

4. Third-party service providers

The app uses these vendors strictly to provide the service:

  • Supabase (database, authentication, file storage) — EU region. See supabase.com/privacy.
  • Meta WhatsApp Business Cloud API (sending payment reminders) — your phone number is shared with Meta only to deliver the message. See whatsapp.com/legal/business-policy.
  • Razorpay (payment processing in India) — only used when you tap “Pay now”. Payment card / UPI details go directly to Razorpay; we never see or store them. See razorpay.com/privacy.
  • Sentry (optional crash reporting) — see sentry.io/privacy.
  • Google Play (app distribution + crash reporting) — see policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Data retention

  • While you are a tenant: all information is retained.
  • After you check out: your tenant record + payment history is retained for up to 7 years for accounting and tax purposes, then deleted on request.
  • Login PIN: can be reset by your landlord at any time. PIN is hashed, never recoverable in plain text.

6. Your rights

You can:

  • See what data we hold about you — ask your landlord, or email the contact below
  • Correct anything inaccurate — name, phone, email
  • Request deletion of your account after you’ve checked out and any outstanding charges are settled
  • Withdraw consent to optional features (e.g. WhatsApp reminders) — tell your landlord

To exercise any of these, contact your landlord directly, or email the address below.

7. Children

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13.

8. Security

  • All connections use HTTPS / TLS
  • Database access is gated by Row-Level Security policies — tenants can only see their own data
  • Login PINs are stored hashed with bcrypt
  • Payment information is never stored by DaboRent — Razorpay handles it directly

9. International users

The app stores data on Supabase’s EU servers. If you are using the app outside the EU, your data is transferred to and stored in the EU.

10. Changes to this policy

We’ll update this page if we change anything material. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect the most recent revision.
