Privacy Policy

Last updated: 31 May 2026

DaboRent is property & tenant management software operated by Cordelia Boaventura Barreto, Pune, India. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Information we collect

WhatWhy
Phone numberTenant login identifier
Full nameSo the property owner can identify the tenant
Email (optional)Backup contact
Room & move-in dateTracking the tenancy
Rent / utility / payment historyShowing what is owed and paid
Meter readingsSplitting utility bills fairly per room
4-digit PIN (hashed)Securing login (bcrypt, never visible to anyone)

We do not collect precise location, contacts, photos, microphone audio, advertising identifiers or biometrics.

2. How we use it

To show tenants what they owe, generate monthly statements, send WhatsApp rent reminders, process rent payments through our payment partner, and help the property owner manage the property. We do not sell data or use it for advertising.

3. Payment data

Online rent payments are processed by our payment partner. Card / UPI / bank details go directly to the payment partner — DaboRent never sees or stores them.

4. Third-party processors

Supabase (database/auth, EU), Meta WhatsApp Business (reminders), our payment gateway (rent collection), Sentry (optional crash logs), Google Play (distribution). Each is used solely to provide the service.

5. Retention

Tenant records and payment history are retained while the tenancy is active and for up to 7 years afterward for accounting/tax, then deleted on request.

6. Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data after settling dues. Contact your property owner or email fromme2007@gmail.com.

7. Security

HTTPS/TLS everywhere, row-level database security so tenants only see their own data, PINs hashed with bcrypt, and no payment credentials stored.

8. Contact

Privacy questions: fromme2007@gmail.com.
