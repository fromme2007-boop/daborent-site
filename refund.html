Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 31 May 2026

This policy explains how refunds and cancellations work for payments made through DaboRent.

1. Nature of payments

Payments made through DaboRent are a tenant's monthly rent and utility share owed to their property owner for accommodation already provided or being provided. They are not purchases of goods.

2. Cancellation

A tenant may cancel a payment before it is completed simply by not authorising it. Once a rent payment is completed, it is applied to the tenant's outstanding dues.

3. Refunds

If a payment is made in error (for example, a duplicate payment or an incorrect amount), the tenant should contact the property owner within 7 days at fromme2007@gmail.com. Verified erroneous or duplicate payments will be refunded to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.

4. How to request a refund

Email fromme2007@gmail.com with the tenant name, room, date and amount of the payment. We will review and respond within 3 business days.

5. Disputes

Any dispute about rent amounts is resolved directly between the tenant and the property owner per their rental agreement.
