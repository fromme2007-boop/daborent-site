About DaboRent

DaboRent is property & tenant management software operated by an individual property owner in Dabolim, Goa, India.

What we are

DaboRent is a private software tool that a property owner uses to manage their own rental rooms and paying-guest tenants. It keeps records of rooms, occupancy, monthly rent, and shared electricity & water usage, and produces a clear monthly statement for each tenant.

Rent collection

Through DaboRent, the property owner collects rent and the tenant's share of utilities from their own tenants. This is a standard landlord-to-tenant rental payment for property the owner manages — not a third-party bill-payment service, marketplace, or aggregator.

Who runs it

DaboRent is operated by Cordelia Boaventura Barreto, an individual property owner based in Dabolim, Goa, India. For any question, email fromme2007@gmail.com.

How tenants get access

DaboRent is invite-only. The property owner adds each tenant; there is no public sign-up. Tenants sign in with their phone number and a PIN to see their own dues and payment history.
